For Employers

A) Tech Recruitment

We place:
  • Full-time employees
  • Part-time staff
  • Contract & remote professionals
Roles covered:
  • Software Engineers (Frontend, Backend, Full Stack)
  • DevOps & System Engineers
  • Cybersecurity Analysts & Architects
  • IT Specialists
Pricing:
  • 20% of annual salary (minimum fee applied)
  • Flexible payment structures
  • Replacement guarantee (90 days)

B) Contract-Based Tech Projects

JRecruits delivers the undeliverable. We partner with world-class international teams to execute any task, no matter the complexity.

Project Types:
  • Websites & mobile apps
  • Custom systems & platforms
  • Cybersecurity audits & protocol implementation

C) Skill-Transfer & International Placement

We facilitate the global movement of talent, both by exporting Ethiopian professionals to international markets and importing specialized foreign expertise into Ethiopia— while operating globally beyond any single country.

Our services include:
  • International placements of Ethiopian talent on fixed-term or long-term contracts abroad
  • Inbound foreign specialists deployed to Ethiopia to fill critical skill gaps
  • Remote global placements, enabling cross-border collaboration without relocation
  • Training-focused engagements, where international experts transfer knowledge and build local capacity when specific skills are not yet available in Ethiopia

Through this model, we ensure immediate access to global expertise while creating long-term, sustainable skill development— connecting Ethiopia to the world and the world to Ethiopia.
